✝ the lore ✝

Nigel is a black cat. He lives in East Van. He has been deemed a folk hero. His pole stands on Woodland Drive, near Woodland Park.

The pole holds many tributes. It is the most successful public art project in East Vancouver and nobody is in charge of it.

Sign the guestbook. Add to the pole. Do not look him directly in the eye.
